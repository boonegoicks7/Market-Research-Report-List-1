<p><p>The biopsy devices market encompasses various techniques used to obtain tissue samples for diagnostic purposes. Stereotactic X-ray guided biopsy utilizes X-ray imaging to precisely target lesions, often in breast tissue. Ultrasound guided biopsy employs real-time ultrasound to locate abnormalities, making it suitable for soft tissue and organ evaluations. MRI guided biopsy uses magnetic resonance imaging for enhanced visualization, particularly effective for detecting tumors in complex anatomical regions. Each method offers unique advantages based on the location and nature of the tissue being examined.</p></p>
