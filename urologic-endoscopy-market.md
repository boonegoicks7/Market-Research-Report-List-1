<p><p>The urologic endoscopy market is primarily categorized into two main types: ureteroscopes and cystoscopes. Ureteroscopes are specialized instruments used to examine the urinary tract, particularly the ureters and kidneys, enabling the detection and treatment of conditions like stones or tumors. Cystoscopes, on the other hand, are designed for visualizing the bladder and urethra, allowing for diagnoses of bladder diseases and urinary tract issues. Both types are essential for minimally invasive procedures, enhancing patient outcomes in urologic care.</p></p>
