<p><p>The de-icing road salt market comprises various types, each with distinct properties and applications. Sodium chloride is the most common and cost-effective option, effectively melting ice. Calcium chloride works at lower temperatures and provides faster results but is pricier. Magnesium chloride is less corrosive and environmental-friendly, making it a popular choice for sensitive areas. Potassium chloride is often used where soil or vegetation health is a concern, balancing effective ice melting with environmental impact. Each type serves specific needs in winter road maintenance.</p></p>
