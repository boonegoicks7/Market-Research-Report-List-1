<p><p>The dried honey market primarily consists of two types: granular and powder forms. Granular dried honey resembles sugar crystals and is often used in baking and as a sweetener, providing a natural alternative to refined sugars. Powdered dried honey, on the other hand, has a finer texture and is easily dissolvable, making it suitable for beverages and dry mixes. Both types retain the natural flavors and nutritional benefits of honey, appealing to health-conscious consumers and food manufacturers alike.</p></p>
