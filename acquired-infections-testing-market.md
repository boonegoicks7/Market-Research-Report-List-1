<p><p>The Acquired Infections Testing Market encompasses two main types: Disease Testing and Drug-Resistance Testing. Disease Testing identifies pathogens causing infections, facilitating effective diagnosis and treatment. This includes tests for viral, bacterial, and fungal infections. Drug-Resistance Testing evaluates the sensitivity of pathogens to specific antibiotics or antiviral medications, crucial for tailoring effective treatment strategies. Both testing types are essential for managing acquired infections, improving patient outcomes, and guiding public health responses to emerging resistant strains.</p></p>
