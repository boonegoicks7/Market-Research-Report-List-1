<p><strong>The USB Industrial Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The USB industrial cameras market primarily consists of two sensor types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality, low noise levels, and excellent light sensitivity, making them ideal for high-resolution imaging applications. In contrast, CMOS cameras offer faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and greater integration capabilities, making them suitable for real-time applications. Both types serve diverse industries, including manufacturing, robotics, and medical imaging, addressing various performance and budgetary needs.</p></p>
