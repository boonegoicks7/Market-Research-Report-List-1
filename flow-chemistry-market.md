<p><strong>The Flow Chemistry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR)</li><li>Plug Flow Reactors (PFR)</li><li>Micro Reactor Systems (MRT)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow chemistry market encompasses various reactor types including Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR), where constant mixing allows for uniform reaction; Plug Flow Reactors (PFR), where reactants move in a continuous plug, ensuring minimal back-mixing; and Micro Reactor Systems (MRT), which utilize small channels for precise control over reaction conditions and enhanced heat transfer. Other types may include batch reactors and hybrid systems. Each type aims to optimize chemical processes for efficiency, scalability, and safety in production.</p></p>
