<p><p>The VR market can be categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based VR uses remote servers to deliver virtual experiences, allowing users to access content from anywhere with an internet connection. This model promotes scalability and reduces hardware costs. In contrast, on-premise VR requires dedicated hardware and software installations at the user's location, offering improved performance and security but necessitating significant upfront investment. Both options cater to different needs in terms of flexibility, control, and resource allocation.</p></p>
