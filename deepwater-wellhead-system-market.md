<p><ul><li>"Conventional Wellhead System"</li><li>"Multi-bowl Wellhead System"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Deepwater Wellhead System market primarily consists of two types: Conventional Wellhead Systems and Multi-bowl Wellhead Systems. Conventional Wellhead Systems are designed for standard drilling operations and typically support a single wellbore, providing essential pressure control. In contrast, Multi-bowl Wellhead Systems feature multiple stages for accommodating various well configurations, allowing for enhanced flexibility and efficiency in deepwater drilling. This advanced design supports multiple completions, optimizing production and reducing costs while managing complex subsurface conditions effectively.</p></p>
