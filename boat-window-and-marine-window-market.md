Fixed windows are immovable and provide a clear view while ensuring structural integrity and weather resistance. Sliding windows can open horizontally, allowing for ventilation while maintaining space efficiency. Hinged windows swing open like a door, offering easy access and ventilation. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to various design preferences and functional needs in marine environments, balancing aesthetics with durability and performance.</p></p>
