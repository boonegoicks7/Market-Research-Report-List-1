<p><p>The manual variable fiber optical attenuators market comprises two primary types: single mode and multimode variable fiber attenuators. Single mode variable fiber attenuators are designed for use in single mode fiber applications, allowing precise control of signal attenuation and ensuring optimal performance in long-distance communication. In contrast, multimode variable fiber attenuators cater to multimode fibers, suitable for shorter distances and higher bandwidths typically found in local area networks. Both types are essential for managing signal strength and quality in fiber optic systems.</p></p>
