<p><p>The Graphite Susceptor Market is primarily segmented into SiC-coated and TaC-coated types. SiC-coated susceptors utilize silicon carbide to enhance thermal stability and efficiency, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. Conversely, TaC-coated susceptors employ tantalum carbide, providing excellent thermal conductivity and mechanical strength. Both types cater to industries such as semiconductor manufacturing and solar cell production, facilitating effective heat distribution and improved process performance. The choice between SiC and TaC coatings depends on specific operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
