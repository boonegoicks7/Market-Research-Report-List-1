<p><strong>The Steel Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Coupling</li><li>Flexible Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel couplings market is primarily divided into two types: rigid couplings and flexible couplings. Rigid couplings are designed to connect two shafts firmly without allowing any misalignment, providing high torque transmission, making them suitable for precision machinery. In contrast, flexible couplings accommodate misalignment and vibrations, allowing for some movement between connected shafts, which is ideal for applications where shaft alignment may vary. Both types serve essential functions in mechanical power transmission across various industries.</p></p>
