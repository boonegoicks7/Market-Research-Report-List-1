<p><p>Power system simulation encompasses various market types, including arc flash simulation, which assesses potential electrical hazards and safety protocols. Short circuit simulation evaluates the current flow during faults, essential for system reliability. Equipment coordination selective simulation focuses on ensuring protective devices operate systematically to isolate faults without disrupting the entire system. Other simulations may include transient stability, harmonics analysis, and load flow studies, all of which are critical for designing, maintaining, and operating safe and efficient power distribution networks.</p></p>
