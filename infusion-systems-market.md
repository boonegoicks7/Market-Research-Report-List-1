<p><p>The infusion systems market consists of various pump types catering to different clinical needs. General purpose or volumetric pumps are versatile devices used for a range of therapies, delivering precise fluid volumes. Patient Controlled Analgesia (PCA) pumps empower patients to self-administer pain relief within prescribed limits. Ambulatory pumps are portable, allowing patients to receive medication while on the move. Other types include syringe pumps and enteral feeding pumps, addressing specific medical requirements across diverse healthcare settings.</p></p>
