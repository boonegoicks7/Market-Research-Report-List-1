<p><strong>The Passenger Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature cord layers arranged at 90 degrees to the tread, providing improved fuel efficiency, better traction, and enhanced durability. They are widely preferred for modern vehicles. Bias tires, on the other hand, have layers crisscrossed at angles, offering a flexible sidewall and a more comfortable ride, making them suitable for older vehicles and specific applications like off-road or heavy-duty uses.</p></p>
