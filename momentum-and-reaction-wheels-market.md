<p><p>Momentum and reaction wheels are critical technologies used for attitude control in spacecraft. Momentum wheels store angular momentum and can adjust a spacecraft's orientation without expelling propellant, while reaction wheels employ the principle of conservation of angular momentum to stabilize and maneuver satellites. These systems are essential for maintaining accurate positioning and orientation in various space missions.</p><p>The Momentum and Reaction Wheels Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast period.
