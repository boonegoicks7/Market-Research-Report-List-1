<p><ul><li>Dynamic Headphones</li><li>Moving Iron Headphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Professional headphones come primarily in two types: dynamic and moving iron. Dynamic headphones use a diaphragm driven by a magnetic coil, delivering rich bass and efficient sound reproduction, making them popular for studio monitoring and casual listening. Moving iron headphones, on the other hand, utilize a small armature to create sound, offering precise audio performance and detail, ideal for professional audio work where clarity is crucial. Both types cater to different preferences and professional needs in audio production.</p></p>
