<p><p>The military surveillance drones market comprises three primary types: Fixed Wing Drones, Rotary Blade Drones, and Hybrid Drones. Fixed Wing Drones are designed for longer flight durations and greater distances, ideal for wide-area surveillance. Rotary Blade Drones, or quadcopters, offer enhanced maneuverability and the ability to hover, making them suitable for tactical missions in confined spaces. Hybrid Drones combine features of both types, providing versatility for various operational environments, including complex terrains and dynamic mission requirements.</p></p>
