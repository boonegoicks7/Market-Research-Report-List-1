<p><p>Traffic radar speed signs are available in three market types based on their power source: battery-powered, AC-powered, and solar-powered. Battery-powered signs offer flexibility and mobility, suitable for temporary installations, but require regular maintenance. AC-powered signs ensure continuous operation and are ideal for permanent locations with access to electricity. Solar-powered signs harness renewable energy, promoting sustainability while providing low operating costs, making them effective for remote areas where electrical infrastructure is limited. Each type serves specific traffic management needs.</p></p>
