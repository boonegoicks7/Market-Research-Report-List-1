<p><strong>The Closed loop Hall Effect Current Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Output</li><li>Threshold Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The closed loop Hall Effect current sensor market is divided into two primary types: linear output and threshold output. Linear output sensors provide a continuous voltage proportional to the measured current, allowing for a precise representation of varying current levels. In contrast, threshold output sensors activate at predetermined current thresholds, providing discrete signals for overcurrent or undercurrent detection. Both types are essential for applications in industrial automation and electronic control systems, facilitating accurate current monitoring and protection.</p></p>
