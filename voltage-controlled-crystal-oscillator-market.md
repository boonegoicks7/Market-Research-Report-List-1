<p><p>The Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillator (VCXO) market features three primary output types: PECL, CMOS, and SINEWAVE. PECL (Positive Emitter-Coupled Logic) offers high-speed performance suitable for telecommunications and data communication applications, providing a differential output. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) outputs are characterized by low power consumption and ease of integration, making them ideal for consumer electronics and portable devices. SINEWAVE outputs deliver analog signals, commonly used in audio and RF applications due to their smooth waveform. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance needs.</p></p>
