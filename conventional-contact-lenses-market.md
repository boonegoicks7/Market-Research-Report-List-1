<p><p>The conventional contact lenses market is segmented into three main types: soft lenses, hard lenses, and rigid gas permeable (RGP) lenses. Soft lenses are made from flexible materials, providing comfort and ease of use, suitable for daily wear. Hard lenses are more durable and offer excellent vision correction but may be less comfortable. Rigid gas permeable lenses combine the benefits of soft and hard lenses, allowing oxygen permeability and excellent visual acuity while retaining durability and comfort.</p></p>
